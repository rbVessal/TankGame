My Game Feature/Upgrade
When the winner dialog box appears, 
the image of the winner's tank also appears below the winner's name.
Also the players are offered a chance to play the game again if they wish to.
If they click on the yes button, then it will start a new game.
If they click on the no button, then it will close all of the dialog boxes.
